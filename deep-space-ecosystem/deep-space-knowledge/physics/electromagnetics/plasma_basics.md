# Plasma Physics

## Definition
Plasma = ionized gas with collective behavior.
Quasi-neutral: n_e ≈ n_i on Debye scale.

## Debye Length
λ_D = √(ε₀·k_B·T_e / (n_e·e²))

Beyond λ_D, charges screen each other.

## Plasma Frequency
ω_pe = √(n_e·e²/(ε₀·m_e))

## Cyclotron Frequency (Hall thrusters)
ω_c = eB/m

## E×B Drift (Hall thruster physics)
v_drift = E×B / |B|²

This is the dominant acceleration mechanism in Hall thrusters.

## Child-Langmuir Law (Ion thruster optics)
j = (4/9)·ε₀·√(2e/m_e)·V^(3/2)/d²

Governs ion current density across accelerating gap.
