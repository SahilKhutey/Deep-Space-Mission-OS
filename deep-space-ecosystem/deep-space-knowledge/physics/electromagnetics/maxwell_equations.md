# Maxwell's Equations

## Gauss's Law (Electric)
∇·E = ρ/ε₀

## Gauss's Law (Magnetic)
∇·B = 0    (no magnetic monopoles)

## Faraday's Law
∇×E = −∂B/∂t

## Ampère-Maxwell Law
∇×B = μ₀J + μ₀ε₀·∂E/∂t

## Constitutive Relations
D = εE
B = μH
J = σE

## Wave Equation (Vacuum)
∇²E = μ₀ε₀·∂²E/∂t²
c = 1/√(μ₀ε₀) = 299,792,458 m/s

## Applications in Space
- Spacecraft communications
- Hall thrusters (E×B drift)
- Ion thruster optics
- Plasma physics
- Radiation belts
