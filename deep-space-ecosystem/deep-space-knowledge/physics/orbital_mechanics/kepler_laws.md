# Kepler's Laws

## First Law (Ellipses)
Planets move in ellipses with the Sun at one focus.

r(ν) = a(1−e²) / (1 + e·cos(ν))

## Second Law (Equal Areas)
The line joining planet and Sun sweeps equal areas in equal times.
dA/dt = h/2 = constant

## Third Law (Harmonic Law)
T² = (4π²/μ)·a³

For two orbits around the same body:
T₁²/T₂² = a₁³/a₂³
