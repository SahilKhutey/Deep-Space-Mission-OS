# Orbital Energy

## Total Specific Energy
ε = v²/2 − μ/r = −μ/(2a)

## Energy Regimes
- ε < 0  →  bound (elliptic) orbit, a > 0
- ε = 0  →  parabolic (escape trajectory)
- ε > 0  →  hyperbolic (unbound), a < 0

## Vis-Viva Equation
v = √(μ·(2/r − 1/a))

At periapsis (r = r_p):
v_p = √(μ·(2/r_p − 1/a))

At apoapsis (r = r_a):
v_a = √(μ·(2/r_a − 1/a))

## Circular Orbit Special Case
For circular orbit (e=0, r=a):
v_c = √(μ/r)
ε = -μ/(2r)
