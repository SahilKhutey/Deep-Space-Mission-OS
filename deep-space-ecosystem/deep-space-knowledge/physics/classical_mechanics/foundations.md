# Classical Mechanics

## Newton's Laws

### First Law (Inertia)
A body remains at rest or in uniform motion
unless acted on by an external force.

### Second Law
F = dp/dt = m·a   (for constant mass)

For variable mass (rockets):
F_thrust = ṁ·v_e + m·dv/dt

### Third Law
For every action there is an equal and opposite reaction.
Foundation of all propulsion.

## Momentum
p = m·v
Conservation: Σp_in = Σp_out (closed system)

## Energy
Kinetic: T = ½mv²
Potential: U = -μ/r
Total: E = T + U = -μ/(2a)  (bound orbit)

## Angular Momentum
h = r × p
For central force: dh/dt = 0 → conservation
|a|² = μ·a(1-e²)  (Kepler's 2nd law equivalent)

## Rigid Body Dynamics
Euler's equations:
I₁ω̇₁ − (I₂−I₃)ω₂ω₃ = M₁
I₂ω̇₂ − (I₃−I₁)ω₃ω₁ = M₂
I₃ω̇₃ − (I₁−I₂)ω₁ω₂ = M₃

Used in spacecraft attitude dynamics.
