# Thermodynamics

## Zeroth Law
If A and B are in thermal equilibrium with C,
then A and B are in thermal equilibrium with each other.

## First Law
dU = δQ − δW
ΔU = Q − W  (closed system)

For propulsion nozzles (adiabatic):
h₀ = h + v²/2 = constant  →  v_e = √(2(h₀ − h))

## Second Law
dS ≥ δQ/T
Entropy of isolated system never decreases.

Carnot efficiency: η = 1 − T_c/T_h

## Third Law
Entropy of perfect crystal at 0 K is zero.

## Combustion
For bipropellant combustion:
Oxidizer + Fuel → Products + Q_heat
Isp = v_e/g₀ = (1/g₀)·√(2·γ·R·T_c/(γ−1)·(1−(p_e/p_c)^((γ−1)/γ)))
