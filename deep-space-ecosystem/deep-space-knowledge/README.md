# Deep Space Knowledge System

The authoritative engineering knowledge foundation for the
Deep Space Mission Planner, Propulsion Simulator, and Digital Twin.

## Philosophy

Engineering Knowledge
        ↓
Physics
        ↓
Mathematics
        ↓
Models
        ↓
Simulations
        ↓
Software
        ↓
Products
        ↓
Business

Every equation, model, and simulation in our software products
is traceable to a validated entry in this repository.

## Hierarchy (8 Levels)

| Level | Domain | Purpose |
|-------|--------|---------|
| L1 | Mathematics | Pure mathematical foundations |
| L2 | Physics | Physical laws governing motion, fields, energy |
| L3 | Aerospace Engineering | Spacecraft, propulsion, GNC |
| L4 | Scientific Computing | Numerical methods, HPC |
| L5 | Systems Engineering | Requirements, trade studies, V&V |
| L6 | Mission Knowledge | Earth-Moon, Mars, asteroid missions |
| L7 | Digital Twin | State estimation, predictive maintenance |
| L8 | AI for Space | Autonomous mission design |

## Validation

Every knowledge entry is:
- Derived from peer-reviewed source
- Validated against a reference solution
- Versioned
- Linked to the software module that implements it
