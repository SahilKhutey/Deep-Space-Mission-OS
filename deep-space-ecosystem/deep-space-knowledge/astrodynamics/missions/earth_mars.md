# Earth → Mars Missions

## Phase Sequence
1. Launch → LEO
2. LEO → TMI (Trans-Mars Injection)
3. TMI → Heliocentric transfer (Hohmann or Lambert)
4. Heliocentric → Mars approach
5. Approach → Mars Orbit Insertion (MOI)
6. MOI → Areostationary / science orbit
7. Optional: EDL (Entry, Descent, Landing)

## Launch Windows
Synodic period: 26 months
Optimal: opposition class
Type I/II transfers: short/long way

| Phase | ΔV (m/s) |
