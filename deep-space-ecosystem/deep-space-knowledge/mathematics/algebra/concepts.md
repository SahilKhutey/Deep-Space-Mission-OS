# Algebraic, Geometric & Trigonometric Concepts

## Linear and Quadratic Equations
Linear: $ax + b = 0 \implies x = -b/a$
Quadratic: $ax^2 + bx + c = 0 \implies x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}$

## Exponential & Logarithmic equations
Rocket equation: $\Delta V = I_{sp} g_0 \ln\left(\frac{m_0}{m_f}\right)$
Inverse: $m_f = m_0 e^{-\Delta V / (I_{sp} g_0)}$

## Spherical Trigonometry
Great-circle navigation: $\cos(d) = \sin(\phi_1)\sin(\phi_2) + \cos(\phi_1)\cos(\phi_2)\cos(\Delta\lambda)$
Used in ground station line-of-sight and ground trace projections.
