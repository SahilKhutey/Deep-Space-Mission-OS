# Vector Calculus for Aerospace

## Gradient
∇f = (∂f/∂x, ∂f/∂y, ∂f/∂z)

Used in:
- Gravity field modeling
- Optimization gradients
- Potential field navigation

## Divergence
∇·F = ∂Fx/∂x + ∂Fy/∂y + ∂Fz/∂z

Used in:
- Maxwell equations (∇·E = ρ/ε₀)
- Mass conservation in fluid dynamics

## Curl
∇×F = (∂Fz/∂y − ∂Fy/∂z, ∂Fx/∂z − ∂Fz/∂x, ∂Fy/∂x − ∂Fx/∂y)

Used in:
- Angular momentum: h = r × v
- Magnetic fields: ∇×B = μ₀J
- Vorticity in fluid mechanics

## Line Integral
∮_C F · dr

Used in:
- Work-energy theorem
- Circulation around airfoils

## Surface Integral
∬_S F · dA

Used in:
- Heat flux through surfaces
- Electric flux (Gauss's law)

## Volume Integral
∭_V f dV

Used in:
- Mass distribution
- Energy density
