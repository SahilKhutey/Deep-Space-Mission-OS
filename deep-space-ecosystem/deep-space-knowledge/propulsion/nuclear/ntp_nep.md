# Nuclear Propulsion

## NTP (Nuclear Thermal Propulsion)
- Fission reactor heats H₂ propellant
- T_max ≈ 2700 K (modern concepts: NERVA, Pewee)
- Isp ≈ 900 s (2× chemical)
- Thrust: 10⁵-10⁶ N

## NEP (Nuclear Electric Propulsion)
- Fission reactor → electricity → ion/Hall thruster
- Power: 100 kW - 1 MW
- Isp: 3000-10000 s
- Specific mass: 5-10 kg/kW

## Specific Power
α = m_system / P_e (kg/kW)
Lower α → better mission performance
